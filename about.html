Alexander Vietmeier is an Emmy-nominated television director based in New York City. For over 15 years, he has specialized in the unique intersection of late-night comedy, live music, and large-scale variety broadcast.

Since 2014, he has served as the Director of Late Night with Seth Meyers at NBC, helming over 1,500 episodes and overseeing the award-winning digital series Corrections.

Alex is a fast thinker who remains calm, cool, and collected in the chair. He thrives on the high-stakes challenge of managing a crew to create great television in real-time. Whether it's the precision of a scripted comedy block or the unpredictability of a live event like the Macy’s Thanksgiving Day Parade, his focus remains on timing, rhythm, and the common goal of the production.

A graduate of Webster University’s School of Communications, Alex balances his deep technical experience with a relentless drive to keep learning.
